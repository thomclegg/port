My name is Thomas Clegg and I'm a Manchester based designer and sometimes front-end developer with a focus on anything digital.

I've seen firsthand how great product design can change people's lives. From working on repeat prescriptions to mortgage applications, the need of the user is paramount to creating great work. I see it as my responsibility to be the 'voice of the user', to put myself in their shoes and ask things like, why are we building this? What need does this work satisfy? What purpose does it serve? Why is it more important than something else?
