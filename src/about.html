My name is Thomas Clegg and I'm a Manchester based designer and sometimes front-end developer with a focus on anything digital.

I got my Masters degree at Hyper Island, a surreal experience which taught me the value of feedback, human-centred design and how karaoke is the universal language of peace. After graduating I moved into advertising for 3 years where I worked my way from Internship to Mid-level and in 2015 I went freelance full-time to pursue my own clients and extend my digital knowledge.

This has led me to work with creative agencies, brands, established business and startups from a wide range of industries, including fashion, retail, tech, fintech, hospitality, property, law, wellbeing, food & drink and many more.
